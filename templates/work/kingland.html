Kingland

Overview

Kingland approached us to redesign is entire brand, something that better represented his name and personality. Previously, his logo was a robot mascot. However, together we decided that a better approach would be to focus on a theme of royalty.

As the scope of this project went beyond branding, the crown logo design was followed up with stream overlay, facecam and rest of the package; and a social media revamp that can broadcast his new branding across all of his social media sites.
