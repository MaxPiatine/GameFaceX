Overview

Alfy is an upcoming Call of Duty stud! Skills are solid, and ambition is there as well. He needed to brand himself with something beyond just call of duty symbols. Proud of his Philippine heritage, this was the route we took. However, we did not loose sight of his Call of Duty gifts – we merged the two and delivered a well personalized logo.

Services
  • Logo Design
  • Twitch Banner
    • Ideation

    Alfy wanted his logo to represent his Philippine heritage – so we decided to use the sun on the flag. Furthermore, we were sought out ways that we can embody an army theme and feel to the logo.

    After several iterations of various different styles, we knew we were getting closer and closer to the final design.

    logo design

    The final logo design was chosen. To add to its aesthetic and army badge theme, a purple gem was place in the center of the ‘A’ and a purple-gold gradient cover each of the eight rays. A great display of both depth and simplicity.
