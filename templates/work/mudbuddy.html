Overview

Mudbuddy, southern hunter and streamer needed something to represent his personality on, and off, of the screen. He hunts duck – but online he is the duck doing the hunting.

We worked out a package that can start his stream and online presence with all the graphics needed for an official brand. From a stream package, logo design, video intro, to a set of subscriber badges; the duck was equipped with everything that he needed to enter the world of twitch.
