GamefaceX - a new beginning

How we got here

In 2016, Hivebranding was created as a graphic design company tailored to the content creators of the gaming world. Since then we’ve helped streamers improve their brands with high quality graphics. Today, we are embarking on a new chapter.


The state of the streaming and graphic design. New channels, technology, games, and platforms has evolved to fit the new demands of the market. We must evolve as well. As GamefaceX, we are setting out to help content creators get their game-face on with designs that empower their stream dreams.


Where we are going

Since the beginning our company’s history, we have set out to become a leading provider of graphics in the market – and develop lasting relationships with every gamer we meet. GamefaceX will be greatly focused on the user experience, technology and accessibility of each of our clients.

We look forward to the new beginnings ahead.
