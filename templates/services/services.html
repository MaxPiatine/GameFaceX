Our process

Our process is tailored to the needs and experience of our clients. With hundreds of projects in our history, we understand what is needed to deliver optimal customer service experience.

1. Place an order

Once you have placed an order, we will get back to you within the next 12 hours via email for details on your project.

2. Discussion

In this step, we discuss the vision for your brand and graphics. Once we have all the information needed, you will receive and ETA (estimated time of arrival) for each step of your project and you will be placed in a queue.

3. Initial Deposit

Once you approve the concept and quote, you will receive an invoice for the payment. 50% paid at the start - 50% at the end. Conditions may vary.

4. Creative

During the creative process, you will be updated on the progress with emails, screenshots and sketches etc. We accept revisions. Please note that revisions may affect delivery time, and (rarely) price.

5. Delivery

In this final step, we will reveal all of your graphics to you. When approved you will receive and email with all of the project files ready to use. Source files are included.
